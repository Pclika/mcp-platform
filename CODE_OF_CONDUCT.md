# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want the Pclika project to be open, practical, and welcoming to developers, makers, educators, and product teams.

Participants should help make this community respectful, constructive, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful
- Be clear and collaborative
- Share feedback in a practical and kind way
- Assume good intent while staying honest about technical issues
- Focus on improving the platform

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or abusive language
- Personal attacks
- Discriminatory behavior
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration
- Sharing private or sensitive data without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions or participation that violate this code.

## Reporting

For now, conduct or community concerns should be reported privately to the project maintainers through the support process described in [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md).
