## Age Requirement

By using this website and purchasing products from PCLIKA, you confirm that you are at least **18 years of age** (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction, whichever is higher).

## Why Age Verification Matters

PCLIKA sells electronic hardware components, development boards, and prototyping kits. While these products are not age-restricted goods, the act of purchasing involves entering into a legally binding contract. Minors may not enter into such contracts in most jurisdictions.

Additionally, some of our products (power supplies, motor drivers, battery modules) involve electrical voltages and currents that require responsible handling by adults.

## Verification Methods

We reserve the right to request age verification if we have reasonable grounds to believe a customer is underage. Verification may include: date of birth confirmation at account registration, and third-party age verification for restricted payment methods.

## Parental Consent

If you are under 18, you may only use this site and purchase products with the involvement and consent of a parent or legal guardian, who agrees to be bound by our [Terms of Service](/policies/terms-of-service).

## Consequences of Misrepresentation

Providing false age information constitutes a breach of our Terms of Service and may result in: immediate order cancellation, account suspension, and reporting to payment providers for fraud.
