## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us remember your preferences, keep items in your cart, and understand how you use our site.

## Types of Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies (Always Enabled)
These are required for the website to function. They enable: shopping cart memory, checkout session security, login authentication, and CSRF protection. Without these cookies, you cannot purchase from our store.

### Analytics Cookies
We use self-hosted Plausible Analytics (privacy-first, no personal data collected) to understand: which pages are most visited, how users navigate the store, and where traffic originates. All data is anonymised and aggregated.

### Marketing Cookies
These cookies enable: personalised product recommendations, abandoned cart reminders (with your consent), and relevant promotional content on our site. We do not use third-party advertising networks or retargeting pixels.

## Cookie Duration

- **Session cookies**: Deleted when you close your browser.
- **Persistent cookies**: Remain for 30 days (cart memory), 12 months (consent preferences), or 26 months (anonymised analytics).

## Your Choices

You can manage cookie preferences at any time by clicking "Cookie Settings" at the bottom of any page. You may also configure your browser to block cookies, but this may prevent you from purchasing from our store.

## Third-Party Cookies

Our payment processors (Stripe, PayPal) may set their own cookies during checkout. These are governed by their respective privacy policies. We use no third-party advertising or tracking cookies.
