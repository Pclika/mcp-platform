## Orders & Payment

**Q: Do you accept purchase orders?**
Yes, for orders over $1,000. Contact sales@pclika.com.

**Q: Can I change or cancel my order?**
You may change or cancel within 2 hours of placing the order. After that, orders enter processing and cannot be modified.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, Apple Pay, and wire transfer for orders over $500.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**
Yes. We use PCI-DSS compliant payment processors. We never store your full credit card details on our servers.

## Products & Compatibility

**Q: Are your products compatible with Arduino?**
Our ESP32-S3 baseboard can be programmed with Arduino IDE, PlatformIO, and ESP-IDF. Most sensor and driver modules use standard I2C/SPI/UART protocols compatible with any microcontroller.

**Q: Do you provide schematics and documentation?**
Yes. All products include datasheets, pinout diagrams, and integration guides. Open-source firmware and reference designs are available on our GitHub.

**Q: Can I request a custom configuration?**
Yes. We offer custom kit assembly for orders of 10+ units. Contact sales@pclika.com with your requirements.

## Shipping & Delivery

**Q: Do you ship internationally?**
Yes. We ship worldwide. See our [Shipping Policy](/policies/shipping-policy) for details.

**Q: How long does delivery take?**
Standard: 3-7 business days (domestic). International: 3-10 business days depending on destination.

**Q: Will I pay customs fees?**
International orders may be subject to import duties. These are the buyer's responsibility.

## Returns & Warranty

**Q: What is your return policy?**
30-day return window for unused items in original packaging. See our [Returns Policy](/policies/returns-refunds) for full details.

**Q: Do your products come with a warranty?**
Yes. 12-month limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. See our [Warranty Policy](/policies/warranty-policy) for details.

**Q: What if I receive a defective item?**
Contact returns@pclika.com within 7 days. We'll provide a prepaid return label and ship a replacement.

## Technical Support

**Q: How do I get technical support?**
Email support@pclika.com or open an issue on our GitHub repository. Include your order number and a detailed description of the issue.

**Q: Do you provide integration support for AI/MCP workflows?**
Yes. Documentation and examples for MCP bridge integration are available on our GitHub and docs site. Community support is available through our GitHub Discussions.
