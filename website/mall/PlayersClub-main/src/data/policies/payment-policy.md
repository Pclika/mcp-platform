## 1. Accepted Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Wire Transfer (for orders over $500).

## 2. Currency

All prices are listed in United States Dollars (USD). If you pay in a different currency via PayPal or credit card, your bank or payment provider will apply their exchange rate. We are not responsible for currency conversion fees.

## 3. Payment Processing

Payments are processed at the time of order. For credit/debit card payments, an authorisation hold is placed immediately. The charge is captured when your order is processed for shipping.

## 4. Billing Information

You must provide accurate and complete billing information. We reserve the right to cancel orders if billing information cannot be verified.

## 5. Failed Payments

If a payment fails, we will notify you via email. You have 3 business days to provide an alternative payment method before the order is automatically cancelled.

## 6. Wire Transfers

For wire transfer orders: Bank details are provided upon request. Orders are processed once funds clear (typically 2-5 business days). The customer is responsible for all wire transfer fees.

## 7. Invoicing

Invoices are provided for all orders. For VAT invoices or customs documentation, contact sales@pclika.com.

## 8. Refunds

Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5 business days of return approval. See our [Returns & Refund Policy](/policies/returns-refunds) for eligibility.

## 9. Security

All payments are processed through PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant payment processors. We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers. Transactions are encrypted with TLS 1.3.
