We want you to be completely satisfied with your PCLIKA hardware purchase. If you are not satisfied, we are here to help.

## 1. Return Window

You have **30 calendar days** from the date of delivery to return an item. After 30 days, we cannot offer a refund or exchange.

## 2. Eligibility Conditions

To be eligible for a return: item must be in original packaging, item must be unused and in the same condition as received, all accessories, manuals and documentation must be included, and a proof of purchase (order number) is required.

## 3. Non-Returnable Items

Opened static-sensitive components (ESD bags breached), custom-configured kits, downloadable software or firmware licenses, and gift cards are non-returnable.

## 4. Return Process

1. Contact us at returns@pclika.com with your order number and reason for return.
2. We will issue an RMA (Return Merchandise Authorisation) number within 2 business days.
3. Ship the item to: Pclika Returns, Protocol Labs Building. Customer pays return shipping unless the item is defective.
4. Once received and inspected, we will process your refund within 5 business days.

## 5. Refunds

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Shipping costs are non-refundable. If you received free shipping, the actual shipping cost will be deducted from your refund.

## 6. Defective or Damaged Items

If you receive a defective or damaged item, contact us within 7 days of delivery. We will provide a prepaid return label and ship a replacement at no cost to you.

## 7. Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. Contact returns@pclika.com to arrange an exchange.
