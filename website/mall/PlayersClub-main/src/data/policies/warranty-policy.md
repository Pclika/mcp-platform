## 1. Limited Warranty

PCLIKA warrants that all hardware products will be free from defects in materials and workmanship under normal use for a period of **12 months** from the date of purchase.

## 2. What Is Covered

Manufacturing defects, component failures under normal operating conditions, firmware bugs affecting core functionality (fixed via update), and DOA (dead on arrival) units.

## 3. What Is Not Covered

Damage caused by: incorrect voltage or polarity, ESD (electrostatic discharge), physical impact or water damage, modification or unauthorised repair, use outside specified operating conditions, and normal wear and tear.

## 4. Warranty Claim Process

1. Contact support@pclika.com with your order number and a description of the issue.
2. Our support team will diagnose the issue and may request photos or diagnostic output.
3. If confirmed as a warranty defect, we will issue an RMA and provide a prepaid return label.
4. Upon receipt, we will repair or replace the product at our discretion and ship it back at no cost.
